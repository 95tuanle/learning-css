Service Master

Service Master revolutionizes home renovation service bookings for homeowners through its user-friendly platform. The platform ensures seamless user management with effortless sign-up, secure sign-out, and strong authentication, along with simplified user profile management capabilities. Users can easily book services, modify bookings, and access their booking history for convenient rescheduling. Service providers benefit from an easy registration process, clear visibility of their services, and effective service management, including cancellations. The technology stack includes an engaging UI with React.js and Angular, a robust backend powered by Node.js and Express.js, consistent design with Bootstrap, Firebase for authentication and real-time data synchronization, Swift for mobile accessibility, and MongoDB for efficient data storage. Experience the future of hassle-free home renovation service management with Service Master.
